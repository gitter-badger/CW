Words with Length Variation

1) Write a test that generates 8 words of length 5 or less characters


# eight-words-five-or-less-letters.rb

require 'cw'

CW.new do
  comment "Eight words, five or less letters (18 WPM)"
  shuffle
  no_longer_than 5
  word_count     8
  wpm            18
end

(download: eight-words-five-or-less-letters.rb)

2) 8 words having size between 2 and 4 letters (15 WPM)


# between-two-and-four.rb

require 'cw'

CW.new do
  comment "8 words having size between 2 and 4 letters (15 WPM)"
  shuffle
  wpm                 15
  no_shorter_than     2
  no_longer_than      4
  number_of_words     8
end
