5 Common Words

1) Write a test that generates 5 common words (test-by-word)

Hint: Enter the following into a text file called five-common-words.rb

You will be tested against the words on a word-by-word basis (rather than letter-by-letter):


# five-common-words.rb

require 'cw'

CW.new do
  comment "5 common words at 12 words per minute"
  shuffle
  word_count 5
  wpm 12
end

(download: five-common-words.rb)

Output:

Hint: The words in red were entered incorrectly!

2) Write a test that generates 5 common words (test-by-letter)

Instead of testing word-by-word, you may also test letter-by-letter.

Hint: You should find this more informative if you are guessing some letters incorrectly


# five-common-words-2.rb

require 'cw'

CW.new do
  comment "5 common words at 12 words per minute"
  shuffle
  wpm        12
  word_count 5
  test_letters    # notice we have added the command test_letters
end

(download: five-common-words-2.rb)

Output:

Hint: The letters in red are were entered incorrectly!

3) Combine both examples into a single test


# five-common-words-3.rb

require 'cw'

CW.new do
  comment "5 common words at 12 wpm (test letters)"
  shuffle
  wpm        12
  word_count 5
  test_letters
end

CW.new do
  comment "5 common words at 12 wpm (test words)"
  shuffle
  wpm        12
  word_count 5
end

(download: five-common-words-3.rb)

4) Five common words (repeat until correct)

hint: using the repeat_word command repeats the word until it is entered correctly


# five-common-words-4.rb

require 'cw'

CW.new do
  comment "5 common words at 12 wpm (test letters)"
  shuffle
  wpm        12
  word_count 5
  repeat_word
end

(download: five-common-words-4.rb)
